# IRE Recruitment Services Website

A modern, responsive website for IRE Recruitment Services with a green, white, and black theme.

## Features

### 🏠 Homepage (`index.html`)
- Hero section with call-to-action buttons
- Services overview with icons
- Office locations display
- Contact information and footer

### 👥 Team Page (`team.html`)
- Team member profiles:
  - **Greg Coleman** - Founder & CEO
  - **Leona Coleman** - Director
  - **Shane Hanrahan** - Manager
- Company values section
- Professional team cards with hover effects

### 📞 Contact Page (`contact.html`)
- Contact form with validation
- Office locations for all branches:
  - Cork (Main Office)
  - Dublin
  - Limerick
  - Croatia
- Interactive map integration
- Contact information cards

### 📄 CV Upload (`cv-upload.html`)
- Comprehensive CV submission form
- File upload functionality
- Personal and professional information sections
- Preferences and consent forms
- Helpful sidebar with tips and information

### 👨‍💼 Employee Forms (`employee-forms.html`)
- Employee portal with access to:
  - Timesheet submission
  - Time off requests
  - Employee handbook
  - Additional forms
- Quick access section with important dates and downloads
