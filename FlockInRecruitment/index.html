About FlockIn Recruitment

Your Global Recruitment Partner for Smarter Hiring

At FlockIn Recruitment, we make hiring simple, friendly, and highly effective. We are a global recruitment partner delivering AI-Driven Offshore RPO and 360° Recruitment Solutions to clients across the USA and Europe — connecting top talent with leading organisations across Tech, Finance, Legal, Engineering, and Healthcare.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between talent and opportunity by combining human expertise with AI-driven precision — helping businesses grow and professionals succeed globally.

Our Vision

To be the go-to global recruitment partner for businesses across the USA and Europe — known for smart, people-focused hiring powered by AI.
