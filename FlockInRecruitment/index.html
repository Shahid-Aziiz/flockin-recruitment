About FlockIn Recruitment

A Recruitment Partner That Actually Gets It Right

At FlockIn Recruitment, we make hiring simple and effective. We work with clients across the USA and Europe, connecting them with the right talent in Tech, Finance, Legal, Engineering, and Healthcare. We handle the full process so our clients spend their time meeting people worth hiring, not sorting through CVs.

To connect the right people with the right opportunities. We combine genuine industry knowledge with smart tools to help businesses grow and professionals find roles that actually fit.

Our Vision

To be the recruitment partner that businesses across the USA and Europe trust and come back to, because we consistently deliver people who perform.

How We Work

No Padding the Shortlist

We only send candidates we'd genuinely recommend. We'd rather send two strong profiles than five weak ones to seem busy.

We Screen Properly

Every candidate we put forward has been spoken to. We check experience, communication, and fit before they ever reach your desk.

Quick Turnaround

Most searches are active within 24 hours of briefing. Urgent roles can be turned around faster when needed.

One Point of Contact

You deal with the same person from brief to placement, not a different consultant every time you call.
