About FlockIn Recruitment

A Recruitment Agency That Works for Both Sides

FlockIn Recruitment is a Lahore-based agency working with businesses and candidates across Pakistan. We handle the full process — sourcing, screening, and shortlisting — so our clients spend time interviewing people worth hiring, not sorting through CVs.

Our Mission

To do right by every client and candidate we work with — honest advice, genuine matches, and no wasting anyone's time.

Our Vision

To be the agency that growing businesses in Pakistan call first — because we've earned that reputation one hire at a time.

What We Do

Permanent placements, contract staffing, executive search, and career guidance — across technology, finance, manufacturing, and more.

How We Work

No Padding the Shortlist

We only send candidates we'd genuinely recommend. We'd rather send two strong profiles than five weak ones to seem busy.

We Screen Properly

Every candidate we put forward has been spoken to. We check experience, communication, and fit — before they reach your desk.

Quick Turnaround

Most searches are active within 24 hours of briefing. Urgent roles can be turned around faster when needed.

One Point of Contact

You deal with the same person from brief to placement — not a different consultant every time you call.

What We Do

Permanent Recruitment

We source, screen, and shortlist for full-time roles across industries. You brief us once — we handle the rest and come back with candidates worth interviewing.

Executive Search

For senior and C-level positions that require discretion and a wider search. We identify and approach candidates who aren't actively looking but are open to the right opportunity.

Candidate Placement

Looking for work? Submit your CV and we'll match you to roles that suit your background. We tell you about every role before putting you forward — no surprises.

Go-To-Market Hiring

Sales, marketing, and business development roles are our speciality. We understand what a strong commercial hire looks like and where to find them in Pakistan's market.

Career Guidance

Not sure what move to make next? We talk through your options honestly — whether that leads to a placement with us or not.

Market Insight

We advise on realistic salary ranges, hiring timelines, and what the current candidate market looks like for your role — based on what we're actually seeing.
